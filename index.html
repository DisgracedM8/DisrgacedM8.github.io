Junior Programmer

& Game Developer

My Name is Jayden Quallio and I am a software developer with experience in creating games using C# and apps using swift, and I have a passion for creating innovative and engaging software solutions.

I began my career as a developer by teaching myself programming and experimenting with different languages and platforms. Through self-study and practice, I became proficient in C# and swift, and I have used these skills to create a variety of games and apps.

I am highly adaptable and always eager to learn new technologies and programming languages. I am committed to producing high-quality software and delivering projects on time and on budget.

I have experience working both independently and as part of a team, and I am able to effectively communicate and collaborate with clients and colleagues.

If you're looking for a developer with a strong work ethic and a passion for creating great software, I would be a great fit for your team.
