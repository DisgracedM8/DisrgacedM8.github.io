Dragon Champs originally called Teacher Fighter was my introduction project as well as my first team based project that I had the chance to work on. Interning at my highschool I was put in charge of taking senior's projects and fixing them up. The biggest project was Teacher Fighter which was a satirical platform fighter similar to Super Smash Bros. It had some of the teachers at the school as playable characters. Alot of the code was spaghetti code and uncommented with lots of asset store assets. I decided that I would make it a lot more optomized while also giving my own twist to it. A couple of other interns joined me in learning Unity and I was head overseeing the production of it. We got the green light to post it to Steam however with the year coming to and end and me graduating we were never able to. There is a playable build on Itch.io. This was my first introduction into game design and what it was like working with a team.
